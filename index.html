conformal-multimodal

LIBRARY v0.3

Distribution-free uncertainty quantification for multimodal ML. Split conformal prediction, conformalized quantile regression, RAPS, Mondrian conditional coverage (group-conditional fairness guarantees), online ACI under distribution shift, and conformal risk control for generalized loss functions. Coverage verified under Gaussian, Cauchy, exponential, and mixture distributions with binomial hypothesis testing at α=0.01.
